About

Hi! I graduated from the University of Utah with a Bachelor's in Computer Science with an emphasis in Games, and I have several years of experience in designing, creating, and iterating on gameplay systems. I love to experiment and make new things, especially when I'm collaborating with other cool and passionate people that want to make something meaningful too.

With an additional background in digital illustration, I bring a strong dual skill-set to my technical role, adopting a visual-first approach to my system designs and communication (+ inevitable doodles to your Slack screens and Miro boards, when appropriate).

Outside of work, I love coffee shops, animals, traveling to new cities and places, playing niche/experimental indie games, and spoiling my dog, Harley. :)
