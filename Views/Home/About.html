About

A cute little page about who I am outside of my projects!

In 2023, I graduated from the University of Utah with a Bachelor's in Honors Computer Science with a Games emphasis. In the coming years, I am planning to earn a Master's in Technical Art in Games.

Since working as a freelance illustrator, I couldn't imagine a life without working in both creative and technical disciplines, hence my love for video games and web development! I am excited to use my passions to continue making innovative, interactive experiences.
